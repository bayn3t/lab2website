Discography

Oasis' discography includes seven studio albums, which are Definitely Maybe (1994), (What's the Story) Morning Glory? (1995), Be Here Now (1997), Standing on the Shoulder of Giants (2000), Heathen Chemistry (2002), Don't Believe the Truth (2005), and Dig Out Your Soul (2008). The band also released two live albums, Familiar to Millions (2000) and Knebworth 1996 (2021), and five compilation albums.
